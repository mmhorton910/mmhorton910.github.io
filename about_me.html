Trip to Ireland

Last week I went to Ireland for the first time with my mom, my sister, and her girlfriend. We spent most of the trip in Dublin, but we did spend one day in Galway. The trip was very fun and I would love to go back to Ireland one day!

Favorite Restaurant

Visit Opium in Dublin if you like Thai food! This is some of the best food I had on my trip to Ireland and the best Thai food I've ever had. They have a passion fruit tart for dessert that I haven't stopped thinking about since I had it.

Favorite Food

  My current favorite food (it changes all the time) is Thai red curry. I like to get mine with chicken and serve it with a side of jasmine rice. I've never tried to make any kind of   Thai food before, but I like to cook so I'd love to try one day!
